Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a physical restaurant?

No, we are a cloud kitchen focused exclusively on delivery and pickup to ensure the highest food quality at your doorstep.

What is your delivery radius?

We currently deliver within a 10km radius of our central kitchen to maintain food temperature and freshness.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you'll receive a link via SMS to track your chef's progress and the delivery driver in real-time.
